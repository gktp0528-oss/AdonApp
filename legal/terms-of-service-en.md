## 6. Buying and Selling Process

### 6.1 Transaction Flow
1. **Agreement:** Buyer and seller negotiate (via chat)
2. **Payment:** Buyer pays into escrow (holding) system
3. **Item Transfer:**
   - **In-person meetup:** Meeting at agreed location; buyer inspects item and shares safety PIN code
   - **Courier delivery:** Seller ships package to courier service; buyer receives and confirms in app
4. **Payment Release:** Escrow system transfers payment to seller
5. **Review:** Both parties rate each other (optional)

### 6.2 Escrow (Holding) Payment System
- **Security:** Buyer's money stays in secure holding until item is received
- **Time Limit:**
  - In-person meetup: 7 days (if not completed, money returns to buyer)
  - Courier: 14 days from seller's shipment
- **Confirmation:** Buyer must confirm receipt within 48 hours
- **Auto-release:** If buyer doesn't respond within 48 hours after receipt, payment auto-releases

### 6.3 Fees and Costs
| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Listing fee | **FREE** |
| Service fee (seller) | **5% of transaction value** (min. 200 HUF) |
| Payment processing fee | **2% + 50 HUF** (buyer) |
| Shipping fee | **Variable** (by courier service) |

**Prices are in HUF (Hungarian Forint).**

**Fee Deduction:** Service fee is automatically deducted from seller's payout.

**ADON does not mediate the buy/sell directly**, so refunds are the seller's responsibility.

**Refund cases:**
- ✅ **Item significantly differs:** If item significantly differs from description (e.g., broken, fake)
- ✅ **Item not received:** For courier, if package doesn't arrive within 14 days

**Refund Process:**
1. Buyer disputes transaction in app (within 48 hours of receipt)
2. ADON support investigates (3-5 business days)
