## 2. What Data Do We Collect?

### 2.1 Registration and Account Data
- **Required:** Name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Optional:** Phone number, profile picture, cover photo, location/city, bio
- **Third-party data:** If you use Google or Apple sign-in, we receive your email and basic profile information

### 2.2 Product Listing Data
- Product title, description, category, price, condition, photos
- Product location (optional, user-provided)
- Upload date and time

### 2.3 Communication Data
- Messages in the chat feature (text, images)
- Reviews and ratings about other users
- Customer support correspondence

### 2.4 Transaction Data
- Purchase and sales history
- Payment information (through secure payment processors)
- Shipping addresses (delivery option only)
- Meetup locations (in-person meetup option only)
- Safety PIN codes (temporary, for transaction verification)

### 2.5 Technical and Usage Data
- IP address, device identifier (UUID), operating system version
- App usage statistics (Firebase Analytics)
- Error reports and diagnostic data
- Browsing history within the app

### 2.6 Location Data
- Approximate location (city-level) for showing nearby products
- **Precise GPS coordinates:** ONLY if you explicitly grant permission (e.g., using map features)

We use your data for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Provision (GDPR Article 6(1)(b) - contract performance)

### 3.2 Security and Fraud Prevention (GDPR Article 6(1)(f) - legitimate interest)

- Filtering fake listings
- Protecting user accounts

### 3.3 AI Features (GDPR Article 6(1)(a) - consent)
- Analyzing product photos (AI)
- Automated price analysis and categorization
- Improving search results with AI
- **Note:** AI analysis is optional; you can disable it in settings

### 3.4 Service Improvement (GDPR Article 6(1)(f) - legitimate interest)
- Improving app performance
- Testing new features
- Optimizing user experience
- Statistical analysis (anonymized data)

### 3.5 Marketing (GDPR Article 6(1)(a) - consent)
- Sending promotional emails (only if you consented)
- Personalized offers
- **Unsubscribe:** You can opt-out anytime from email notifications

## 5. How Long Do We Store Your Data?

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Product listings | Until listing/account deletion |
| Chat messages | Until account deletion + 90 days |
| Transaction data | 7 years (accounting and legal requirements) |
| Reviews | Until account deletion (may remain anonymized) |
| Technical logs | 90 days |
| Marketing consent | Until withdrawn |

- Legal obligations (e.g., accounting records: 7 years)

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15-22)

Under EU GDPR, you have the right to:

### 6.1 Right of Access (Article 15)
Request a copy of your data we process.
**How:** support@adon.app or Settings > Download My Data (coming soon)

### 6.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)
Correct inaccurate data.
**How:** Settings > Edit Profile

### 6.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)
Request deletion of your data (except for legal obligations).
**How:** Settings > Delete Account or support@adon.app

### 6.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)
Request suspension of data processing (e.g., for disputed data).

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
Request your data in machine-readable format (JSON).
**How:** support@adon.app

### 6.6 Right to Object (Article 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interest (e.g., marketing).
**How:** Settings > Disable Notifications

### 6.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77)
You can file a complaint with the Hungarian National Authority for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (NAIH):
- **Website:** [naih.hu](https://naih.hu)
- **Email:** ugyfelszolgalat@naih.hu
- **Address:** 1055 Budapest, Falk Miksa utca 9-11., Hungary

**Response time:** We fulfill requests within 30 days (complex cases: max. 60 days).

We protect your data using:

- **Encryption:** Passwords encrypted with bcrypt; data transfer via HTTPS/TLS
